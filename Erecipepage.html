Spaghetti Marinara is a delicious Italian dish that transports your taste buds to Tuscany.

It features perfectly cooked spaghetti topped with a flavorful marinara sauce made from ripe tomatoes, garlic, and aromatic herbs. Each bite is bursting with flavor, enhanced by grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves. Served with crusty garlic bread, this dish is a satisfying indulgence that will leave you wanting more.

Whether you're in the mood for comfort food or a gourmet treat, Spaghetti Marinara is sure to satisfy.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of spaghetti
  • 4 cups of marinara sauce
  • 4 ripe, juicy tomatoes
  • 4 cloves of fresh garlic
  • 1 tablespoon of dried basil
  • 1 tablespoon of dried oregano
  • A pinch of rosemary
  • 1/2 cup of finely grated Parmesan cheese
  • A handful of fresh basil leaves
  • 1 loaf of crusty bread
  • 4 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 cloves of garlic for the bread

Preparation

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions until it's al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. While the spaghetti is cooking, prepare the marinara sauce. In a large saucepan, combine the marinara sauce, tomatoes, garlic, dried basil, oregano, and rosemary. Simmer over low heat for several hours to infuse the flavors.
  3. Preheat your oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) for the garlic bread. Slice the loaf of bread and spread butter and minced garlic on each slice. Arrange the slices on a baking tray and bake until golden brown.
  4. Once the sauce has simmered for several hours and your garlic bread is ready, it's time to assemble the dish. Toss the spaghetti in the marinara sauce until it's fully coated.
  5. Dish out the spaghetti onto plates, top with a generous sprinkle of finely grated Parmesan cheese while it's still hot so the cheese will melt into the pasta.
  6. Garnish with fresh basil leaves for added color and flavor.
  7. Serve the spaghetti with a side of crusty garlic bread.
  8. Enjoy your Delicious Spaghetti!
